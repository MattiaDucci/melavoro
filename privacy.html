We here at Kryax SRL respect your privacy. Here is a little explanation of when and why we might collect your data, and what we do with it.

Collection and Use of Personal Information:

Kryax SRL does not collect personal information on our Site or Apps. However, Kryax SRL uses Apple Inc.’s billing software to process purchases of, and sometimes paid subscriptions to, Kryax SRL Apps made through the iTunes Store. As a result, when arranging your purchase via your mobile device or through the iTunes Store, you may be asked to provide personal information to Apple Inc., such as your name, email address or zip code (for billing purposes).

This information is only collected as a means of supporting our paid subscription programs available for certain Apps. Unless the Law forces us, we will not share any personal information with any unaffiliated third party for any purpose... ever. We will not send promotional emails or special offers, we will not contact you for market research, and we will not provide your information to third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. In addition, Kryax SRL will never sell your personal information to any unaffiliated third party... ever.

Kryax SRL may disclose or publish indirect information about you that is not intended to specifically identify you (non-personally identifiable information, such as device IDs, IP addresses and cookies) for industry analysis, demographics, marketing, analytics, advertising, and other business purposes.
